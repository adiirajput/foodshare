How FoodShare Works

Three steps to connect surplus food with those who need it most.

01
🍽️

Donor Lists Food

Restaurants, households, or events list surplus food with quantity, location, and expiry details.

02
🤝

Recipient Claims

NGOs, shelters, or individuals browse and instantly claim available food near them.

03
🚚

Pickup & Done

Recipient collects food from the pickup location. Donor marks it complete. Zero waste!
